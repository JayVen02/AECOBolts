AECO BOLTS 2025

Bahay ni KuyAECO

This is a 3 day and 2 night leadership bootcamp made for AECO members and its partner student organizations. This program helps student leaders grow, starting from self-leadership to leading others, so they are ready to face real-life challenges with skills, kindness, and good values.

The event includes learning sessions and hands-on leadership practice. It is inspired by Filipino community life and the format of Pinoy Big Brother. During the camp, the venue is set up like a "house" where the students, called housemates, need to think, work together, and face different challenges as one team.

Through BOLTS 2025, AECO continues its mission to build strong and caring leaders. It gives student leaders a space to reflect on themselves, learn servant leadership, and create meaningful connections, just like housemates walking together in one journey of growth.

Speaker Modules

Module 1: Why Student Leadership Matters?

Day 1 - Formation Session

Focus: Leadership beyond titles - influence, initiative, and servant leadership

This session defines leadership in the Atenean context, helping participants recognize their role in shaping communities and identifying their "why" for stepping up as student leaders.

Module 2: How Successful People Lead

Day 1 - Leadership Development

Focus: Leadership development stages and leadership styles

Overview of proven leadership models to help participants assess their current leadership level and create a growth path moving forward in the Atenean context.

Module 3: Leadership Starts with Self & Others

Day 2 - Self-Leadership

Focus: Self-leadership traits and collaborative leadership skills

Develops self-awareness and personal responsibility as the foundation of leadership, covering initiative, empathy, flexibility, and team dynamics.

Module 4: Problem Solving & Critical Thinking

Day 2 - Decision Making

Focus: Structured problem-solving and adaptive thinking

Teaches structured problem-solving skills, decision-making confidence, and leadership under pressure through real-time challenges and frameworks.

Module 5: Understanding COA & Funds Transparency

Day 3 - Financial Responsibility

Focus: COA auditing and transparent budgeting

Practical introduction to transparent and accountable financial management in student organizations, covering documentation and common mistakes to avoid.

Module 6: Turning Vision into Action

Day 3 - Project Management

Focus: Project management and execution skills

Equips participants with tools to transform leadership ideas into executable projects, covering SMART goals, budgeting, timelines, and delegation.
